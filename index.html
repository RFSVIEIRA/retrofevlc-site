Project Overview

What is RetroFeVLC?

RetroFeVLC is a modern, actively developed fork of the popular open-source RetroFE frontend, rebuilt with VLC media library support and optimized for Windows.

By replacing GStreamer with VLC, we've achieved superior video handling, better image rendering, and rock-solid stability for long gaming sessions.

The VLC Advantage

Native handling of 4K content, animated WebP/GIF support, and zero memory leaks. It's the engine your retro collection deserves.

Pure Performance

Designed for speed. Browse thousands of games with zero lag and beautiful transitions.

Built for 2026

Standing alongside the best frontends in the world, RetroFeVLC offers the perfect balance of visual flair and ease of use.

Quick Installation

  1. Download the Full Release package.
  2. Extract to your preferred drive (e.g., D:\RetroFeVLC).
  3. Launch Start.exe to begin.
  4. Use the System Tray icon to access the Settings and Controls editors.
